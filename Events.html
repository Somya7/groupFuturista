Blockchain an emerging technology has attracted the interest from Energy firms, Start-ups, Financial institutions, Technology developers, the Academic community and National Governments as well. In recent times more people continue to generate their own wind and solar energy, smart grids now allow energy producers that also consume a portion of the energy they produce, to sell their excess peer to other people.
The use of Blockchain in the energy sector means that every single energy flow and business activity can be securely recorded on decentralized storage of every data. The industry is changing, and the widespread use of Blockchain in energy will ensure that cleaner energy will be produced with consumers having greater control and having more options to choose from.
Group Futurista in collaboration with industry’s key players and innovative thinkers is organizing ‘Blockchain Summit for Energy’ in October 2019, Germany. A best way to combine market expertise and achieve the scale which is essential to launch a digital transaction platform that could transform the way we all do business!

Precision Medicine is an emerging approach for disease prevention. It takes into account a patient’s gene variability, surroundings and his lifestyle to map out a treatment plan. Based on a patient’s predicted response and the risk of developing a disease, the scientific industry can cater the need of a patient by fabricating the right product.
Current statistics show that by 2025, a 100 million to 2 billion human genomes will be sequenced, which will bring novel targeted therapies, improved patient outcomes and will be cost effective.
“Future of Personalised Medicine Summit for Oncology” is an event which will be organized by Group Futurista, to help Entrepreneurs, Scientists and Professionals to discourse the current trends in Scientific and Technological developments.
The Summit will be held on the 14th and 15th November, 2019 in Munich, Germany. Industry leaders will enlighten us on the latest technologies such as CRISPER Cas9, Next Generation Sequencing, Big Data and AI which plays a crucial role in Precision Medicine.

IoT Technology along with Big Data Analytics is well on its way to transform Supply Chain Management. It will lend a helping hand to businesses from being reactive to proactive. The future of IoT is predicted to lead a 15% productivity increase in the Supply Chain industry. Many logistic experts are using new resources to enhance their supply networks, minimizing cost and looking for opportunities to generate revenue. The Next Generation Digital Supply Chain will create a disruption and will require companies to rethink the way to design their Supply Chain.
‘Future of Supply Chain Summit’ organized by Group Futurista will be held in December 2019 in Amsterdam. Industry leaders from around the globe will come together and discuss how Supply Chain can be made faster, flexible, granular and precise.

Just as electric light became competitive with gas light a century ago, renewable energy and electric cars are becoming competitive with technologies based on fossil fuels. However, for new technologies to become truly transformative there is a need to develop a new generation of batteries to power them.
Battery technologies are central to delivering significant advances in a broad range of industries ranging from electrical vehicles to electronic devices for renewable energy. The increasing reliance on battery storage is driving enormous demand. This has catapulted battery technology to the top of the priority list for many players, leading to a massive boom in investment. Overall, battery applications are projected to become $90 billion plus market. While smart phones, smart homes and even smart wearables are growing ever most advanced, they are still limited by power.
Group Futurista is all set to organize a ‘Future of Batteries Summit’ in February 2020 in France. Participants will get an update on how latest technology can improve energy density, safety, battery lifespan and aid in cost reduction, thus making sure players in the market take a long-term view and ensure that they are focusing on the right technologies and business models for their organizational success.

The Next Generation Food Technology embraces a simple concept of minimizing the use of resources, pollution and waste, while feeding a population of 9.6 billion. The UN Food and Agricultural Organisation predicts that the agricultural Industry will need to produce 70% more food, while using only 5% more land to feed the drastically growing population.
Digital agriculture can provide solutions to issues, which will not only change how farmers farm but will essentially, transform every part of the agribusiness value chain. Precision Farming one of the key aspect of digital agriculture enables farmers to accurately measure, map and manage any variations in a field to significantly increase yields while lowering the production cost.
Group Futurista is organizing a ‘Future of Food Summit’ in March 2020 in Lisbon, where futurists and innovative minds will acquaint us with novel business strategies, product designs and customer preferences associated with Digital Agriculture and methods to overcome the major challenge of sharing and using data.

Making payments and managing bank accounts is no longer a task associated with wallets. Modern consumer demands everything at the click of a button. On an average, an individual has to handle 27 different login and passwords to access online services. Not surprisingly, password fatigue is become a real problem and users wish to have a secure digital identity.
To transform creative ideas into reality and to incorporate such innovative designs into business processes, Group Futurista is organizing “Future of Digital Onboarding and Customer Experience Summit 2.0” a networking event which will be held in April 2020, London. Delegates from all around the globe will discuss ways to transform the legacy onboarding processes by implementing novel technologies to deliver a faster, smoother and better customer experience. The leaders at the Summit will show us a clear picture and the advantages of adopting various tools and analytics that recognizes market changes.
