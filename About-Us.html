Group Futurista

The 21st century has seen a complete change in the way life operates on this planet. We are at the cusp of the 4th Industrial Revolution that will be based on the code, data or information based social and economic system. In the digital age of intelligent machines, rampant mechanization and automation of all biological and physical phenomena, technology is literally a body invader, penetrating consciousness, and reshaping our perception in cybernetic loops of information. Human subjectivity has become so deeply and inextricably embedded with technology that the 'post human' is born at the interstices of data and flesh, erasing the traditional demarcation between man and machine. This calls for alternative ways of thinking about humanity and its environments, new way of embracing change and a new way of understanding ourselves.

Group Futurista is a leading Global Events Company creating its own emerging Technology Summits, where creativity fosters and innovations flourish. We believe in the power of networking and connecting with innovative thinkers and brightest minds from Industries, Academia and Government organizations at a global level.

We bring together key influencers to share case studies, research and information on cutting-edge technologies such Artificial Intelligence, Blockchain technology, and IoT. Innovative Keynotes delivered by the Industry leaders integrate the best practices and the challenges that business leaders should be aware of to scale up their business.

Our Mission and Vision is to leave behind a strong legacy that will drive sustainable growth and maintain the balance of life on this planet for our future generations to cherish.

Futurista Events are also open to new ideas and collaborations from people from diverse backgrounds.

Please send us a mail at jennifer@groupfuturista.com for further queries and suggestions.




Our Board of Directors


Eline Chivot

Senior Policy Analyst at Center for Data Innovation ElineChivot is a senior policy analyst at the Center for Data Innovation, a research institute affiliated with the Information Technology and Innovation Foundation (ITIF)—the world’s top ranked science and technology policy think tank. Based in Brussels, Eline focuses on European technology policy issues with a strong emphasis on the impact of laws and regulations on the data economy in the EU, and on how policymakers can promote digital innovation. Eline received Masters degrees including from Sciences Po in France, in political science, economics, strategic management and business administration. She speaks English, French, and Dutch.


Sasha Borovik

CFO and General Counsel at Zaka CFO and General Counsel at Zaka, which is a London and Kigali based startup. Since graduating from Harvard Law School in 1999, Sasha been working as an international attorney and entrepreneur for some of the best corporations and law firms in Silicon Valley, Seattle, Washington DC, Munich, Paris and London. Representing Microsoft, he handled some of the biggest technology deals in history with institutions, such as NATO, UN and the European Commission. He also has been involved in a great number of public-private partnerships. As a member of several management teams, he was leading companies’ legal and growth strategy across the world. Sasha speaks seven languages and is admitted to professional bars in the EU and in the State of New York.
